STEP 2: THE EVALUATION PROCESS
The core of our service lies in our meticulous and scientific evaluation of your gold jewelry. This process involves several critical stages:

AUTHENTICATION: The first step is to confirm the item is genuine gold. Our appraisers employ a combination of non-destructive and minimally invasive techniques. This may include visual inspection for hallmarks (e.g., 10K, 14K, 18K, 22K, 24K, .417, .585, .750, .916, .999), magnet tests (gold is non-magnetic), and precise acid tests. For advanced authentication and purity analysis, we utilize X-ray Fluorescence (XRF) technology, which provides a highly accurate, non-damaging elemental breakdown of the metal composition.

PURITY ASSESSMENT (KARATAGE): Once authenticated, the gold's purity, or karatage, is determined. Karat (K) is a measure of gold's fineness, with 24K representing 99.9% pure gold. Common purities include 10K (41.7% gold), 14K (58.5% gold), 18K (75% gold), and 22K (91.6% gold). Our appraisers precisely identify the karat of each item, as this directly impacts its value.

WEIGHT MEASUREMENT: Each item or group of items of the same karatage is then weighed using highly calibrated, certified scales. We typically measure in grams (g) or pennyweights (dwt), which are standard units in the precious metals industry. This precise measurement is crucial for accurate valuation.

DEDUCTION FOR NON-GOLD COMPONENTS: If the jewelry contains non-gold elements such as gemstones, diamonds, enamel, or base metal components (e.g., steel springs in clasps), these elements are carefully separated or their weight is estimated and deducted from the total weight of the gold. Unless the stones themselves hold significant independent value, the offer will be based solely on the net gold weight. Our appraisers will transparently explain any deductions.

CURRENT MARKET PRICE INTEGRATION: The value of gold is intrinsically linked to the fluctuating global spot price of gold, which changes daily, sometimes hourly. KG_PITKIN utilizes real-time market data to ensure that the offer presented reflects the most current and competitive gold prices. This ensures fairness and transparency in our pricing model.
