1. Initial Assessment:
Upon your visit to KG_FREEPORT, our experienced staff will conduct an initial visual inspection of your diamond. This preliminary step helps to identify the item and understand its general characteristics, setting the stage for a more detailed evaluation.

2. Gemological Evaluation (The 4Cs and Beyond):
Our certified gemologists will then perform a comprehensive assessment using professional tools such as a jeweler's loupe, microscope, diamond tester, and UV light. This evaluation adheres to the globally recognized "4Cs" of diamond grading, along with other critical factors:
Carat: The weight of the diamond, where 1 carat equals 200 milligrams. This is a primary determinant of value.
Cut: The quality of a diamond's cut determines its brilliance, fire, and sparkle. This is often considered the most important C, as a well-cut diamond will appear more lively. Our experts assess polish, symmetry, and proportions.
Color: Diamonds are graded on a color scale from D (colorless) to Z (light yellow or brown). Colorless diamonds are the most rare and valuable.
Clarity: This refers to the presence or absence of inclusions (internal flaws) and blemishes (external imperfections). Graded from Flawless (FL) to Included (I3).
Beyond the 4Cs: We also consider fluorescence, the presence and nature of any treatments (e.g., HPHT, irradiation), the diamond's origin (if known and certified), and the presence of any reputable certification (GIA, AGS, IGI, EGL). For mounted diamonds, the value of the precious metal setting is also assessed.

3. Market Analysis:
Once the gemological characteristics are established, our team analyzes current market conditions. This includes demand for specific diamond sizes, shapes, and qualities, as well as prevailing resale prices. We leverage real-time market data to ensure our offers are competitive and reflect the current global diamond market.

4. Offer Generation:
Based on the comprehensive gemological evaluation and current market analysis, KG_FREEPORT will present you with a no-obligation cash offer. Our experts will clearly explain how the value was determined, providing full transparency throughout the process.
